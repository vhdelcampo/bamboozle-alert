###Definition  
bam·boo·zle bamˈbo͞ozəl  
*alternate forms: bamboozleing, bamboozlement, bamboozled*  
+to fool or cheat (someone). Ex. Lordtuts promised to tattoo a meme on his butt but instead bamboozled redditors for karma.
+to confound or perplex. Ex. The increasing number of false promises on me_irl has bamboozled the community's belief in the sanctity of trust.|
Bamboozling is related swindling, trickery, tomfoolery, catfishing, and OP not delivering.|
